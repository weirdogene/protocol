4. Maintenance

Duration and target index during maintenance (reference).

A. Duration

  • Default: at least 24 hrs
  • Duration can be varied according to patient's characteristics (eg, cerebral edema, uncontrolled status epilepticus) and clinician’s decision.

B. Target index

Item Target
Head position30-degree head elevation
Glucose140–180 mg/dL
SaO2≥ 95%
PaCO235–45 mmHg
Blood pressureMean arterial pressure ≥ 65 mmHg or systolic arterial pressure ≥ 90 mmHg
Volume status Maintain an euvolemic state if possible: hypovolemia may lead to cerebral ischemia and hypervolemia may lead to IICP by brain edema.

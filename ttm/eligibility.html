1. Eligibility

Inclusion Criteria

Targeted temperature management may be considered in patients who meet

  • Age ≥ 18 years
  • More than 20 consecutive minutes of ROSC
  • Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) < 13 and lack of meaningful response to verbal commands after sustained ROSC

Exclusion Criteria

  • Obvious or suspected pregnancy
  • Active bleeding, platelet count < 70,000/µL, aPTT > 40 sec, INR > 1.5, or known coagulopathy
  • Major operation within 7 days
  • Life-threatening infection, such as severe sepsis
  • Persistent hypotension: systolic blood pressure < 80 mmHg despite adequate fluid resuscitation, vasopressor/inotropic support, or ECMO
  • Baseline body temperature < 30℃ before initiation of hypothermia

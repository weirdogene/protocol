Scoring rules

Formula for calculation of rCAST

1.0 × (Initial rhythm score) + 2.0 × (Witness/until ROSC time score)
+ 2.5 × (pH score) + 0.5 × (Lactate score) + 4.5 × (GCS M score)
= rCAST score points
